Our Story: Where Passion Meets the Pour

Established in 2025, Bean & Brew was founded on the simple belief that a great cup of coffee can make any day better. We are committed to sourcing fair-trade beans and baking our pastries fresh daily. We believe in quality, community, and the perfect balance of sweetness and strength.

✨ Today's Featured Specials

  • The Caramel Cloud: Our signature latte with homemade caramel and sea salt foam.
  • Prosciutto & Fig Croissant: A flaky pastry with savory-sweet filling.
  • Ethiopian Yirgacheffe: A light-roast pour-over with bright, fruity notes.
